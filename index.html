Drink and Eat as Milwaukeeans Do!

This page is to boast about the beer and food industry in Milwaukee, WI

One very interesting this about Milwaukee, is that we have what is known as a traveling beer garden. These beer gardens are run by Sprecher Brewery, and rotate throughout the Milwaukee County Public Parks.
